- When hit points drop below 0 hit points, or when taking damage while below 0 hit points, become unstable
- At the beginning of your turn while unstable, roll a Constitution save of DC 15
  - after three successful saves you become stable
  - each failed save applies Fatigued^[Fatigued creatures takes a level of exhaustion]
- Incapacitated^[An incapacitated creature can’t take actions or reactions.] while below 0 hit points
